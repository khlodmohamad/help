Individual Sector

HELP provides domestic labors in conformity with demands of individuals commencing from the private driver, domestic helper through male / female nurse, private chief, farmer as well as guardian. Further, the company may provide various skilled workers for house maintenance, periodically. HELP has 18 branches covering 10 cities across the kingdom providing different packages starting from 3 months contract, 6 months, 12 months, and 24 months. In addition, the client has the option to renew contract. Branch locations are identified by the Company based on a number of factors including population, income profile and consumer insight using data gathered through clients’ requests through Maharah’s web portal and mobile application. At the end of 2018, Maharah developed its mobile application to serve the clients of individual sector that don’t need to go anywhere for choose, request, pay and get the service. All that doable from the application. HELP has proven its capability of serving more than 65,000 household client from selection and recruitment of resources, provide comprehensive training, until the process of deploying the manpower where the company aims for diversifying of resources in terms of nationalities and professions to meet customer demand. Moreover, conducting training sessions for the selected resources, improve their skills set to ensure meeting customer specifications, and satisfactions.
